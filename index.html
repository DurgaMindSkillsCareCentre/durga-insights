✦ About D. Durga ✦

Healing Minds. Empowering Lives.

Chennai's trusted name in psychiatric care, counselling, and personal transformation.

D. Durga is the Founder and CEO of Durga Psychiatric Centre and Durga MindSkillsCare Centre, located in T Nagar, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. With a rich multi-disciplinary background spanning nursing, alternative medicine, business administration, HR management, and social work, D. Durga brings a truly 360° approach to mental wellness.

Her practice covers the full spectrum — from clinical psychiatric care and evidence-based therapy for conditions like depression, anxiety, OCD, trauma, and sleep disorders, to empowering individuals through soft skills training, life coaching, and corporate wellness programmes. Every patient and participant receives personalised, compassionate, and culturally sensitive support.

D. Durga is especially known for her certificate courses covering sexual health, practical psychotherapy, LGBTQ+ wellness, couples counselling, perinatal mental health, and nursing psychology — making professional-grade mental health education accessible to all.
